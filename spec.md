# Shri Shyam Trans Logistics Corporate Website

## Overview
A premium professional corporate website for Shri Shyam Trans Logistics, a transportation and 3PL logistics company. The website showcases the company's services, values, and contact information with a sophisticated, high-trust design that emphasizes professionalism and reliability.

## Design Requirements
- Premium corporate navy blue, white, and orange accent color palette
- Professional, high-trust layout with sophisticated typography and spacing
- Enhanced responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Professional imagery throughout all sections for visual depth
- Dark navy overlays with excellent contrast for text readability
- Polished animations and transitions for premium brand presence
- Prominent company logo integration in header and About Us sections
